Think of me?  
Merely several times  
Of this feeling,  
Would make me  
As glaring  
As the rainbow after rain.  

Wish I were gusts of fresh breeze  
At every sunrise and sunset,  
Over and over again,  
Arouse ripples in ur heart.  
Longly resounded  
Deep inside ur soul.
